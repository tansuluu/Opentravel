Welcome to Kyrgyzstan

Discover the spectacular alpine lakes and vistas of remote Kyrgyzstan, tucked high up in the rugged mountain ranges of Central Asia. Nomadic traditions are alive and well in this mountainous nation; take a private Kyrgyzstan tour with a local guide and experience this ancient way of life firsthand. Your expert personal guide knows the best-kept secrets and places favoured by the Kyrgyz locals, including spectacular Ala-Archa National Park, with its glacier-topped alpine grandeur, not far from the capital city of Bishkek. Horse-riding and trekking are excellent ways to explore around scenic Lake Issyk-Köl, or the summer pastures of the country's many nomadic herders. Haggle for bargains in Osh, a thousands-year old bazaar located along the fabled Silk Road, and sleep in a yurt under the stars.
